Professionally Used AI Tools

At AI Python Solutions, we explore the best AI tools for professional applications. Below is a curated list of tools, their uses, and pricing, along with methods to integrate them into powerful agent tools for automation and productivity.

AI Chatbots and Assistants

  • ChatGPT (OpenAI): Conversational AI for content creation, coding, and customer support.
    Pricing: Free plan; Plus: $20/month; chatgpt.com.
  • Claude (Anthropic): Safe, value-aligned AI for writing and coding.
    Pricing: Free plan; Pro: $20/month; anthropic.com.
  • Perplexity: AI research assistant with web search and PDF analysis.
    Pricing: Free plan; Pro: $20/month; perplexity.ai.
  • Gemini (Google): Multimodal AI for SEO, research, and Google Workspace integration.
    Pricing: Free plan; Workspace plans vary; gemini.google.com.

AI Writing and Content Creation

  • Jasper: Content creation for blogs and ads with internet research.
    Pricing: $39/month; jasper.ai.
  • Copy.ai: Generates marketing copy and emails.
    Pricing: Free plan; Pro: $36/month; copy.ai.
  • Notion AI: Writing and organization within Notion.
    Pricing: Free plan; $10/user/month; notion.so.

    Pricing: Free plan; $12/month; descript.com.

AI Design and Image Generation

  • Canva AI: Design tools for social media and presentations.

Integration Tools

To create powerful agent tools, use platforms like Zapier, n8n, or Agent.ai to connect AI tools into cohesive workflows. These platforms automate tasks by linking APIs and triggering actions across apps.

  • Zapier: Connects 5,000+ apps for automation.
    Example: Link ChatGPT to Canva for automated content and design workflows.
  • n8n: Open-source tool for complex automations.
    Example: Combine Claude and Synthesia for text-to-video pipelines.
  • Agent.ai: Builds custom AI agents.
    Example: Integrate Perplexity, Jasper, and Canva for marketing campaigns.

Example Workflow: Marketing Campaign Automation

A small business can automate a product launch campaign using:

  • Perplexity: Research trending topics (free).
  • Jasper: Generate social media posts ($39/month).
  • Canva AI: Create visuals (free).
  • Zapier: Automate content scheduling ($19/month).
  • Buffer: Publish posts (free).
